# Legal Review Notes

The repository currently keeps the root [LICENSE](LICENSE) as MIT.

Immediate release recommendation:

- keep the root [LICENSE](LICENSE) unchanged for this launch window
- strengthen patent, trademark, notice, attribution, and enforcement materials
- do not switch to Apache-2.0 or another license during the synchronized launch
  push unless owner and legal review explicitly approve it

What is already implemented in-repo:

- root legal pack files for notice, attribution, trademark, patent posture, and
  enforcement intake
- packaging gates so those files ship with release artifacts
- redistribution guidance that asks public mirrors and reposts to preserve
  source reference where reasonably practicable

What still needs owner or legal sign-off:

- whether MIT remains the long-term repository license
- whether source-reference requirements stay as project policy and trademark
  guidance, or move into a different license framework later
- whether the project wants a separate patent grant, patent promise, or patent
  retaliation clause
- whether `security@openclaw.ai` remains the intake address for non-security IP
  notices, or a dedicated legal address should replace it
