## 10. PDPA Data Protection and Consent  
## 十、PDPA 个人资料保护与同意声明

### 10.1 Purpose of Collection 收集目的

By signing this Form, the Student (and, where applicable, the guardian) acknowledges that the personal data provided will be collected, used and disclosed by Maple Education Pte. Ltd. (“Maple”) and its authorized partners for the following purposes:  
签署本表即表示学生（及在适用情况下的监护人）知悉并同意：Maple Education Pte. Ltd.（“Maple”）及其授权合作方可为以下目的收集、使用及披露所提供的个人资料：

- providing study abroad counselling and advisory services;  
  提供留学咨询及规划服务；  
- assessing suitability for programs and institutions;  
  评估学生与各类项目及院校的匹配度；  
- preparing, submitting and managing applications to educational institutions;  
  准备、递交及管理对各院校的申请；  
- facilitating visa, enrollment and pre-departure arrangements;  
  协助签证、入学及行前准备；  
- complying with legal, regulatory and risk management requirements.  
  符合法律、监管及风险管理要求。

### 10.2 Data Disclosure 资料披露

Personal data may be disclosed to:  
个人资料可能披露给：

- relevant partner institutions and their appointed service providers;  
  相关合作院校及其指定服务提供方；  
- Maple’s affiliated entities and professional advisers (on a need-to-know basis);  
  Maple 的关联公司及专业顾问（在工作需要范围内）；  
- government agencies and authorities where required by applicable laws and regulations.  
  法律法规要求的政府部门及监管机构。

### 10.3 Retention and Protection 保留期限与安全

Maple will retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes set out above or as required by law, after which the data will be anonymised or securely deleted.  
Maple 仅在为实现上述目的所合理必要或法律要求的期限内保留个人资料，期满后将对资料进行匿名化处理或安全删除。

Maple will adopt reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss or misuse.  
Maple 将采取合理的技术和组织措施，防止个人资料被未经授权访问、丢失或滥用。

### 10.4 Rights of the Data Subject 资料主体权利

Subject to applicable laws, Students may:  
在适用法律允许范围内，学生有权：

- request access to and correction of their personal data held by Maple;  
  要求查阅及更正 Maple 所持有的其个人资料；  
- withdraw consent for the use or disclosure of their personal data, subject to legal and contractual restrictions and reasonable notice;  
  在符合法律及合同约定、并给予合理通知的前提下，撤回对其个人资料使用或披露的同意；  
- raise any concerns or complaints regarding data protection to Maple or, where appropriate, to the relevant data protection authority.  
  就个人资料保护问题向 Maple 或相关监管机构提出疑问或投诉。

Requests or enquiries may be directed to:  
相关请求或咨询可发送至：
