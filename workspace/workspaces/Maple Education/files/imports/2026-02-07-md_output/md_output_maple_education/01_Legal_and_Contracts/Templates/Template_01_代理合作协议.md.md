## 1. Purpose and Background  
## 一、合作目的与背景

1.1 The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a long-term, transparent and compliant cooperation framework under which Party A refers prospective students to Party B for international education consulting and placement services.  
本协议旨在建立一套长期、透明且合规的合作框架，据此由甲方向乙方推荐有意出国（含新加坡及其他国家/地区）就读的潜在学生，由乙方提供国际教育咨询与院校申请服务。

1.2 The Parties intend to build a sustainable partnership based on professional service standards, compliance with applicable laws (including Singapore law and Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (“**PDPA**”)), and fair sharing of commission income received from partner institutions.  
双方希望在专业服务标准、遵守适用法律（包括新加坡法律及《新加坡个人资料保护法》（PDPA））以及公平分享合作院校支付佣金的基础上，建立可持续的业务合作关系。

## 2. Definitions  
## 二、术语定义

For the purpose of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set out below:  
除非本协议另有约定，下列术语具有如下含义：

2.1 **“Student”** means any individual referred by Party A to Party B, who has a genuine intention to pursue education abroad and whose information is registered with Party B in accordance with this Agreement.  
“**学生**”：指由甲方推荐给乙方、具有真实出国就读意向，并依本协议约定在乙方系统中完成登记的自然人。

2.2 **“Partner Institution”** means any educational institution with which Party B maintains a cooperation or agency arrangement and from which Party B may receive commission income.  
“**合作院校**”：指与乙方存在合作或代理关系，并可能向乙方支付佣金收入的教育机构。

2.3 **“Base Commission”** means the commission amount actually payable by the relevant Partner Institution to Party B for a specific Student and program, before any sharing with Party A.  
“**基准佣金**”：指合作院校就特定学生及项目实际应向乙方支付的佣金金额（未与甲方分成前的金额）。

2.4 **“Commission Rebate”** means the portion of the Base Commission that Party B agrees to share with Party A under this Agreement.  
“**返佣**”：指乙方根据本协议约定，在收到基准佣金后按约定比例支付给甲方的部分。

2.5 **“Successful Enrollment”** means that a Student has: (i) received an unconditional or appropriately conditioned offer; (ii) accepted the offer; (iii) paid the required tuition or fees; and (iv) commenced studies at the Partner Institution for at least the minimum period required for commission to accrue under the institution’s policy.  
“**成功入读**”：指学生已：(i) 获得无条件或适当条件录取通知；(ii) 正式接受录取；(iii) 支付规定学费或费用；且 (iv) 在合作院校实际入读并达到该院校政策下产生佣金的最低就读时间。

## 3. Appointment and Relationship  
## 三、授权与合作关系

3.1 Party B hereby appoints Party A as a non-exclusive referral partner to identify and refer Students to Party B for designated programs and Partner Institutions.  
乙方现委任甲方作为非排他性推荐合作方，为乙方就指定项目和合作院校识别并推荐学生。

3.2 Party A acts as an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as creating an employment relationship, partnership, joint venture or legal representation between the Parties. Party A is not authorized to bind Party B or make any commitments on behalf of Party B unless expressly agreed in writing.  
甲方以独立承揽人身份开展合作，本协议不构成双方之间的雇佣关系、合伙、合资或法律意义上的代理关系。除非经乙方书面明示授权，甲方无权代表乙方作出任何承诺或使乙方承担任何法律责任。

3.3 Party B may appoint other referral partners in the same territory, and Party A acknowledges that this Agreement does not grant exclusivity unless otherwise agreed in a separate written addendum.  
乙方有权在同一市场区域与其他推荐合作方签约，除非双方另行以书面形式明确约定排他性，甲方承认本协议并不赋予其任何独家权利。

## 4. Territory and Target Market  
## 四、合作区域与目标市场

4.1 Unless otherwise agreed, Party A may refer Students located in the People’s Republic of China and other mutually agreed markets to Party B, subject always to applicable local laws and regulations.  
除非双方另有约定，在遵守当地适用法律法规前提下，甲方可就居住在中华人民共和国及双方协商一致的其他市场的学生向乙方进行推荐。

4.2 Party A shall not actively solicit or market to any jurisdiction where such activity would breach applicable laws, regulatory requirements or sanctions.  
甲方不得在任何其营销或招揽行为将违反适用法律、监管要求或制裁措施的司法辖区内主动进行推广或业务开发。

## 5. Obligations of Party A (Referral Partner)  
## 五、甲方（推荐代理方）的义务

5.1 Party A shall use reasonable commercial efforts to identify Students who are suitable and genuinely interested in programs offered by Partner Institutions served by Party B.  
甲方应以合理商业努力，识别适合并真实有意向申请乙方合作院校项目的学生。

5.2 Before referring a Student, Party A shall:  
在推荐学生前，甲方应：

(a) obtain the Student’s prior consent to share their personal data with Party B and the relevant Partner Institutions for the purposes described in this Agreement; and  
(a) 事先取得学生同意，将其个人资料提供给乙方及相关合作院校，用于本协议项下所述目的；以及  

(b) conduct an initial assessment of the Student’s academic background, language ability and study plan, and provide accurate information to Party B.  
(b) 对学生的学业背景、语言能力及留学规划进行初步评估，并向乙方提供准确的信息。

5.3 Party A shall submit each Student’s details using Party B’s designated registration channel (system, online form or email), in accordance with Party B’s current standard operating procedures.  
甲方应按照乙方不时更新的标准操作流程，通过乙方指定的登记渠道（系统、在线表单或电子邮件）提交每位学生的信息。

5.4 Party A shall not:  
甲方不得：

(a) make any false, misleading or unapproved statements regarding Party B, Partner Institutions or programs;  
(a) 对乙方、合作院校或相关项目作出虚假、误导性或未经批准的陈述；  

(b) offer discounts, guarantees or commitments in the name of Party B;  
(b) 以乙方名义提供任何折扣、保证或承诺；  

(c) engage in any conduct that may damage the reputation or legitimate interests of Party B or any Partner Institution.  
(c) 从事任何可能损害乙方或合作院校声誉或合法权益的行为。

## 6. Obligations of Party B (Service Provider)  
## 六、乙方（服务提供方）的义务

6.1 For Students duly referred and registered by Party A, Party B shall provide professional education consulting and application services, including but not limited to program advisory, document guidance, application submission, and follow-up with Partner Institutions.  
对于经甲方合规推荐并完成登记的学生，乙方应提供专业的留学咨询与申请服务，包括但不限于项目规划、材料指导、申请递交以及与合作院校的后续对接。

6.2 Party B shall keep Party A reasonably informed of key milestones in each Student’s application process (such as application submitted, additional documents requested, offer issued, visa outcome, enrollment).  
乙方应就每位学生申请过程中的关键节点（如递交申请、补件要求、录取结果、签证结果、入学情况）向甲方进行合理的信息反馈。

6.3 Party B shall maintain up-to-date information on Partner Institutions’ admission requirements, tuition fees and commission policies, and shall inform Party A of any material changes that may impact Students or commission arrangements.  
乙方应维护有关合作院校录取要求、学费标准及佣金政策的最新信息，并在该等信息发生重大变化且可能影响学生或返佣安排时，合理及时地通知甲方。

6.4 Party B shall handle all Student data in accordance with PDPA and other applicable data protection laws, and shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss or misuse.  
乙方应依照PDPA及其他适用数据保护法律处理所有学生资料，并采取适当的技术与组织措施，防止个人资料被未经授权访问、丢失或滥用。

## 7. Student Registration and Attribution  
## 七、学生登记与归属

7.1 A Student shall be attributed to Party A if: (i) Party A has first submitted the Student’s details via the designated channel; and (ii) Party B has confirmed in writing (including via system notification or email) the registration of such Student under Party A.  
若满足以下条件，学生即归属于甲方名下：(i) 甲方通过指定渠道首次提交该学生信息；且 (ii) 乙方已书面确认（包括系统通知或电子邮件）该学生登记在甲方名下。

7.2 In case multiple partners submit the same Student, the Student shall be attributed to the partner whose submission is first confirmed by Party B, unless the Student expressly designates otherwise in writing.  
如多家合作方向乙方提交同一学生，除非该学生以书面形式明确指定合作方，否则学生归属于乙方优先确认登记的一方。

7.3 Party B may, after reasonable consultation, re-assign a Student from Party A to another partner or to Party B directly if there is clear evidence of the Student’s written request or regulatory constraints requiring such reassignment.  
如基于学生书面请求或监管要求确有必要，乙方可在与甲方合理沟通后，将学生从甲方名下调整至其他合作方或直接由乙方服务。

## 8. Commission, Payment and Taxes  
## 八、佣金、结算与税务

8.1 For each Student who achieves Successful Enrollment at a Partner Institution, Party B shall pay Commission Rebate to Party A in accordance with the commission structure attached as **Appendix A (Commission Schedule)**, as amended from time to time by mutual agreement.  
对于每一位成功入读合作院校的学生，乙方应依本协议附件一《返佣项目清单》（经双方不时协商更新）所载的佣金结构，向甲方支付返佣。

8.2 Commission Rebate shall only become due and payable after Party B has actually received the corresponding Base Commission from the relevant Partner Institution. If no Base Commission is received for any reason, Party B shall have no obligation to pay Commission Rebate for that Student.  
返佣仅在乙方实际收到相关合作院校支付的基准佣金后方告发生并应支付。如因任何原因合作院校未向乙方支付基准佣金，则乙方无义务就该学生向甲方支付返佣。

8.3 Subject to Clause 8.2, Party B shall settle Commission Rebate to Party A within [●] calendar days after receipt of the Base Commission and completion of necessary internal reconciliation.  
在符合第8.2条前提下，乙方应于收到基准佣金且完成必要内部对账后的[●]个日历日内，向甲方结算相应返佣。

8.4 All commission amounts are expressed as gross amounts. Each Party shall be responsible for its own taxes, duties and charges arising from the Commission Rebate, except as otherwise required by law. Party B may withhold taxes where required by applicable law and shall provide reasonable supporting documents to Party A.  
所有佣金金额均为税前金额。除非法律另有强制规定，各方应自行承担因收取返佣而产生的税费。若适用法律要求乙方代扣代缴税款，乙方可依法扣缴并向甲方提供合理的扣缴证明文件。

8.5 Commission shall be paid in [SGD / other agreed currency]. Where currency conversion is required, the Parties shall adopt the exchange rate specified in Appendix A or, failing that, the prevailing interbank rate on the date of remittance.  
返佣以[新加坡元/双方约定的其他币种]支付。如需进行币种兑换，双方应采用附件一约定的汇率，若附件未约定，则以汇款当日适用的银行间市场汇率为准。

## 9. Data Protection and PDPA Compliance  
## 九、数据保护与PDPA合规

9.1 Each Party acts as an independent data controller in respect of the Student personal data it processes under this Agreement.  
就本协议项下各自处理的学生个人资料而言，双方各自独立承担数据控制者责任。

9.2 Party A shall:  
甲方应：

(a) obtain valid and informed consent from each Student (and, where required, from their parent/guardian) for the collection, use and disclosure of their personal data to Party B and relevant Partner Institutions for the purposes of counselling, application, enrollment and post-enrollment support; and  
(a) 就为留学咨询、申请、入学及后续服务目的而向乙方及合作院校收集、使用及披露学生个人资料一事，从学生（及必要时其家长/监护人）取得有效且明示的知情同意；以及  

(b) keep appropriate records of such consents and promptly notify Party B if a Student withdraws or modifies consent.  
(b) 妥善记录该等同意，并在学生撤回或变更同意时及时通知乙方。

9.3 Party B shall:  
乙方应：

(a) process personal data strictly for legitimate and reasonable business purposes related to this Agreement, including counselling, application processing, compliance, risk management and service improvement;  
(a) 仅为本协议相关的合法合理业务目的（包括咨询服务、申请处理、合规管理、风险管理和服务优化）处理个人资料；  

(b) retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for such purposes or as required by law or regulators, after which the data shall be anonymised or securely deleted;  
(b) 在为实现上述目的所合理必要或法律及监管要求的期限内保留个人资料，期满后应对资料进行匿名化处理或安全删除；  

(c) put in place appropriate technical and organizational safeguards against unauthorized access, accidental loss or unlawful processing; and  
(c) 通过适当的技术和组织措施，防止个人资料被未经授权访问、意外丢失或违法处理；以及  

(d) provide Students with reasonable means to access, correct and, where applicable, withdraw consent in accordance with PDPA requirements.  
(d) 依PDPA要求，为学生提供合理渠道以行使查阅、更正及（在适用情形下）撤回同意等权利。

9.4 Where personal data is transferred across borders (for example, from China to Singapore or from Singapore to other jurisdictions), each Party shall ensure that such transfers comply with PDPA and any applicable local data export restrictions, including by taking reasonable steps to ensure that the recipient provides a standard of protection comparable to that under PDPA.  
如需进行跨境个人资料传输（例如从中国向新加坡，或从新加坡向其他司法辖区传输），各方应确保该等传输符合PDPA及其他适用的资料出境规定，并采取合理措施确保接收方提供与PDPA相当的保护水准。

9.5 In the event of a material personal data breach affecting Students referred by Party A, the affected Party shall, without undue delay, notify the other Party and cooperate in good faith on any required notifications to Students or regulators.  
如发生对甲方推荐学生产生影响的重大个人资料泄露事件，受影响的一方应在不无故拖延的前提下通知另一方，并就对学生或监管机构的必要通报与应对措施进行善意协作。

## 10. Confidentiality  
## 十、保密义务

10.1 Each Party shall treat as confidential all non-public information relating to the other Party’s business, Students, Partner Institutions, commission arrangements and operational processes obtained in connection with this Agreement.  
各方应对因本协议而获知的对方业务信息、学生信息、合作院校信息、返佣安排及运营流程等非公开信息予以严格保密。

10.2 Confidential information shall not be disclosed to any third party except: (i) to employees, agents or professional advisers on a need-to-know basis; (ii) as required by law or regulatory authorities; or (iii) with the prior written consent of the disclosing Party.  
除以下情形外，保密信息不得向任何第三方披露：(i) 向在履行本协议过程中因工作需要知情的员工、代理人或专业顾问披露；(ii) 依据法律或监管机构要求必须披露；或 (iii) 经信息披露方事先书面同意。

## 11. Compliance, Anti-Bribery and Sanctions  
## 十一、合规、反贿赂与制裁

11.1 Each Party shall conduct its activities under this Agreement in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to anti-bribery, anti-corruption, anti-money laundering and education-related regulations in its jurisdiction.  
各方在本协议项下开展活动时，应遵守所有适用法律法规，包括但不限于各自所在司法辖区的反贿赂、反腐败、反洗钱及教育相关法规。

11.2 Neither Party shall offer, promise or give any improper financial or other advantage to any person in connection with this Agreement.  
任何一方不得就本协议事项向任何个人或实体提供、承诺或给予不当的金钱或其他利益。

11.3 Each Party represents that it is not subject to any sanctions that would prohibit it from performing this Agreement, and shall promptly notify the other Party if this representation ceases to be true.  
各方声明其目前不受任何导致无法履行本协议的制裁措施约束，如该声明不再真实，应立即通知对方。

## 12. Limitation of Liability  
## 十二、责任限制

12.1 To the maximum extent permitted by law, neither Party shall be liable to the other Party for any indirect, consequential, special or punitive damages arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.  
在法律允许的最大范围内，任何一方均不就因本协议引起或与本协议相关的任何间接损失、后果性损失、特殊损失或惩罚性赔偿向对方承担责任。

12.2 Party B does not guarantee that any Student will obtain admission, visa approval or scholarship, as such outcomes are subject to decisions by third-party institutions and authorities.  
乙方不对任何学生是否获得录取、签证批准或奖学金作出保证，上述结果取决于第三方机构及主管部门的独立决定。

12.3 Without prejudice to Clause 12.1, Party B’s total aggregate liability arising out of this Agreement in any contract year shall not exceed the total Commission Rebate actually paid to Party A in that contract year.  
在不违反第12.1条的前提下，乙方在任一合同年度内就本协议承担的累计责任总额不应超过其在该年度实际向甲方支付的返佣总额。

## 13. Term and Termination  
## 十三、期限与终止

13.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of [●] years, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Clause 13. It shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either Party gives at least thirty (30) days’ prior written notice of non-renewal.  
本协议自生效日起生效，初始期限为[●]年，除依本条提前终止外持续有效。如双方未在期满前至少三十（30）日以书面形式提出不续约通知，本协议自动续约一年。

13.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience by giving not less than thirty (30) days’ prior written notice to the other Party.  
任何一方均可通过向对方提前不少于三十（30）日发出书面通知，无需说明理由终止本协议。

13.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect by written notice if the other Party:  
如出现以下情形，任何一方可书面通知对方立即终止本协议：

(a) commits a material breach of this Agreement which is not remedied within fourteen (14) days after written notice; or  
(a) 严重违反本协议且在收到书面通知后十四（14）日内未予纠正；或  

(b) becomes insolvent, enters into liquidation or is subject to any similar proceedings.  
(b) 出现无力清偿、进入清算或发生任何类似程序。

13.4 Termination shall not affect any accrued rights, outstanding Commission Rebate in respect of Students who have already achieved Successful Enrollment, or clauses which by their nature are intended to survive termination (including but not limited to Clauses 8, 9, 10, 11, 12 and 15).  
本协议的终止不影响任何一方在终止前已产生的权利与义务，包括但不限于对已成功入读学生尚未结算的返佣。按照其性质在协议终止后仍应继续有效的条款（包括但不限于第8、9、10、11、12及15条）不因终止而失效。

## 14. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution  
## 十四、适用法律与争议解决

14.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Singapore.  
本协议受新加坡法律管辖并依其进行解释。

14.2 Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be resolved through friendly consultations between the Parties. If no resolution is reached within thirty (30) days, the dispute shall be finally resolved by arbitration administered by the Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC) under the SIAC Rules in force at the time of the arbitration. The seat of arbitration shall be Singapore. The language of arbitration shall be English and Chinese, with English prevailing in case of inconsistency.  
因本协议引起或与本协议相关的任何争议，双方应首先通过友好协商解决。如在三十（30）日内协商未果，该争议应提交新加坡国际仲裁中心（SIAC），按照届时有效的《SIAC仲裁规则》最终裁决。仲裁地为新加坡，仲裁语言为英文及中文；如两种语言存在不一致，以英文为准。

## 15. Miscellaneous  
## 十五、其他条款

15.1 **Entire Agreement**  
本协议及其附件构成双方就本协议事项达成的完整协议，并取代双方此前就同一事项达成的所有口头或书面谅解与安排。  

15.2 **Amendments**
