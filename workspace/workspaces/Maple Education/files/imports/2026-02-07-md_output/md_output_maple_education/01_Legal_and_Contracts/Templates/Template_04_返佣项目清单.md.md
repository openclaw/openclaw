# Template 04 – Referral Commission Schedule  
# 模板 04 – 代理返佣项目清单

> Internal use only. This Schedule forms an integral part of the Referral Commission Cooperation Agreement between Party A and Party B.  
> 仅供内部使用。本清单为甲乙双方《代理合作协议》的组成部分。

## 1. General Principles 一、总则

1.1 This Commission Schedule sets out the indicative commission structures for key partner institutions and programs served by Maple Education Pte. Ltd. (“Party B”).  
本返佣清单列明 Maple Education Pte. Ltd.（“乙方”）当前主要合作院校及项目的参考返佣结构。

1.2 Actual commission amounts and percentages remain subject to the final written confirmation and settlement from each Partner Institution. This Schedule does not constitute a public offer to any third party.  
实际佣金金额与比例以各合作院校的书面确认与最终结算为准。本清单不构成向任何第三方的公开要约。

1.3 Party B may adjust the commission structure from time to time due to institutional policy changes, exchange rate movements or market conditions. Any material changes affecting Party A’s Commission Rebate shall be communicated to Party A in writing.  
由于院校政策调整、汇率波动或市场变化，乙方有权不时调整返佣结构；如该等调整对甲方返佣权益产生实质影响，乙方应以书面形式通知甲方。

---

## 2. Key Concepts 二、关键概念

- **Base Commission 基准佣金**：合作院校按每位学生实际支付给乙方的佣金金额。  
- **Rebate to Party A 返佣给甲方**：乙方在收到基准佣金后按约定比例或固定金额支付给甲方的部分。  
- **Currency 币种**：如无特别说明，以 SGD 新加坡元计价。  
- **Payment Trigger 支付触发条件**：一般为学生成功入读并就读达到合作院校规定的最低期限，且乙方实际收到基准佣金。

## 4. Volume Incentives and Special Campaigns  
## 四、年度奖励与专项活动

4.1 Party B may, at its discretion, offer additional volume-based incentives (e.g. per-student bonus after a certain number of Successful Enrollments in a calendar year). Such incentives, if any, shall be set out in a separate written notice or addendum.  
乙方可视业务情况自行决定是否提供基于年度送生数量的额外奖励（例如：年度成功入读人数达到一定数量后，按每生额外奖励固定金额）。如有该等奖励，乙方应以单独书面通知或补充协议形式予以载明。

4.2 Any temporary promotional campaign (e.g. short-term increased commission for specific programs) shall clearly specify the applicable period, eligible Students, and settlement rules, and shall not be interpreted as a permanent change to the baseline commission structure.  
任何短期推广活动（例如针对特定项目的临时提高返佣比例）应清晰载明适用期间、适用学生范围及结算规则，不应被视为对基准返佣结构的永久性调整。

---

## 5. Reconciliation and Reporting  
## 五、对账与记录

5.1 Party B shall maintain internal ledgers recording Base Commission received from each Partner Institution and Commission Rebate paid to Party A by Student and by program.  
乙方应建立内部台账，按学生与项目记录各合作院校向乙方支付的基准佣金以及乙方向甲方支付的返佣金额。

5.2 Party A is encouraged to maintain its own student and commission tracking sheet. The Parties agree to reconcile records at reasonable intervals (e.g. quarterly), and to cooperate in good faith to resolve any discrepancies.  
鼓励甲方自建学生及返佣跟踪表。双方同意在合理间隔（例如每季度）进行对账，并本着诚信原则合作解决任何差异。
