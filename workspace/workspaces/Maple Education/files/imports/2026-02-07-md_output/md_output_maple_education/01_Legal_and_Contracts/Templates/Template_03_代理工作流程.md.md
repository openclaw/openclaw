# Template 03 – Standard Procedure for Agent Cooperation  
# 模板 03 – 代理合作标准流程

> This document is an operational guideline supporting the Referral Commission Cooperation Agreement between Party A and Party B. It does not, by itself, constitute a standalone legal obligation or service commitment.  
> 本文件为甲乙双方《代理合作协议》的配套操作指引，本身不单独构成法律义务或服务承诺。

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope  
## 一、目的与适用范围

1.1 This Procedure sets out the standard end-to-end workflow for cooperation between the referral partner (**Party A**) and Maple Education Pte. Ltd. (**Party B**) in relation to student referrals, application processing, enrollment and commission settlement.  
本流程用于规范推荐合作方（**甲方**）与 Maple Education Pte. Ltd.（**乙方**）在学生推荐、申请办理、入学落地及返佣结算等环节的标准作业流程。

1.2 It applies to all Students referred by Party A to Party B under the Referral Commission Cooperation Agreement, unless otherwise agreed in writing for specific projects or campaigns.  
本流程适用于甲方在《代理合作协议》框架下推荐给乙方的所有学生，除非双方就某些专项项目或活动另行以书面形式约定差异化流程。

---

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities  
## 二、角色与责任

2.1 **Party A (Referral Partner)** focuses on student acquisition, preliminary qualification and local communication.  
**甲方（推荐代理方）**主要负责学生获客、初步筛选及本地沟通对接。  

2.2 **Party B (Service Provider)** focuses on professional counselling, application strategy, document quality control, submission to Partner Institutions and upstream relationship management.  
**乙方（服务提供方）**主要负责专业咨询、申请策略制定、材料质控、向合作院校递交申请以及上游关系维护。  

2.3 Both Parties shall comply with applicable laws (including data protection and education regulations) and with the Referral Commission Cooperation Agreement at all times.  
双方在履行本流程时，应始终遵守适用法律法规（包括数据保护及教育相关法规）以及《代理合作协议》的约定。  

---

## 3. Stage I – Lead Registration  
## 三、阶段一：线索登记

3.1 **Initial Contact 初次接触**  
- Party A engages prospective Students via its own channels (online/offline).  
  甲方通过自身线上/线下渠道获取意向学生。  
- Party A briefly introduces Party B’s positioning, major cooperation institutions and core value proposition.  
  甲方向学生简要介绍乙方定位、主要合作院校及核心价值主张。  

3.2 **Information Collection 信息采集**  
- Party A conducts an initial assessment of the Student’s background, intentions and budget.  
  甲方对学生的基本背景、留学意向及预算进行初步评估。  
- Party A collects basic personal data and study preferences in line with Party B’s latest Student Information Form (Template 02).  
  甲方按乙方最新《学生信息收集表》（模板02）的字段采集学生信息。  

3.3 **PDPA Consent PDPA 同意**  
- Before submission to Party B, Party A obtains the Student’s explicit consent (and guardian’s, where applicable) for personal data to be shared with Party B and Partner Institutions.  
  在向乙方提交前，甲方应取得学生（及适用情况下其监护人）对个人资料共享至乙方及合作院校的明示同意。  

3.4 **Lead Registration 线索登记**  
- Party A submits the Student’s details via Party B’s designated channel (system / online form / email).  
  甲方通过乙方指定渠道（系统/在线表单/邮箱）向乙方登记学生信息。  
- Once confirmed by Party B in writing (system notification or email), the Student is attributed to Party A for commission purposes.  
  乙方书面确认（系统通知或邮件）后，该学生在返佣归属层面计入甲方名下。  

---

## 4. Stage II – Document Preparation  
## 四、阶段二：材料准备

4.1 **Document Checklist 清单确认**  
- Party B provides a tailored document checklist based on the Student’s target country, institution and program.  
  乙方根据学生的目标国家、院校及项目，提供对应的材料清单。  
- Party A communicates this checklist to the Student and clarifies priority, sequence and timelines.  
  甲方向学生解释材料优先级、准备顺序及时间要求。  

4.2 **Collection and Pre‑Screening 收集与初审**  
- Party A assists the Student in gathering documents (transcripts, certificates, language scores, passport scans, financial proof, CV, PS/RL etc.) in soft copy.  
  甲方协助学生收集软性材料：成绩单、毕业证/在读证明、语言成绩、护照扫描、资金证明、简历、个人陈述/推荐信等。  
- Party A conducts a basic completeness and legibility check before forwarding documents to Party B.  
  甲方在转交乙方前，对材料的完整性与清晰度进行基础检查。  

4.3 **Quality Control and Drafting 质控与撰写支持**  
- Party B reviews key documents and may provide templates or drafting guidance for PS/RL and other narrative components.  
  乙方对关键材料进行质量审核，并可为个人陈述/推荐信等文字材料提供模板或撰写指导。  
- Party A supports Student to refine drafts in line with Party B’s comments, ensuring consistency and authenticity.  
  甲方协助学生根据乙方反馈修改文书，确保内容真实且前后逻辑一致。  

---

## 5. Stage III – Application Submission and Follow‑Up  
## 五、阶段三：递交与跟进

5.1 **Internal Review 内部审核**  
- Party B consolidates all documents and performs a final compliance check against Partner Institution requirements.  
  乙方汇总材料，并依据合作院校要求进行最终合规审核。  
- Where necessary, Party B may request clarifications or additional documents via Party A.  
  如有需要，乙方可通过甲方向学生索取补充说明或附加材料。  

5.2 **Application Submission 申请递交**  
- Party B is responsible for completing application forms and submitting the application package to Partner Institutions through official channels.  
  乙方负责填写院校申请表，并通过官方渠道向合作院校递交完整申请材料。  
- Party A shall not submit parallel applications to the same institution for the same Student without Party B’s written consent.  
  未经乙方书面同意，甲方不得就同一学生向同一院校重复或平行递交申请。  

5.3 **Status Updates 状态更新**  
- Party B monitors application status (submitted / pending / additional documents / interview / decision) and updates Party A on key changes.  
  乙方跟踪申请进度（已递交/待处理/补件/面试/结果）并就关键变化向甲方更新。  
- Party A is responsible for relaying updates to the Student in a timely manner and coordinating any required actions.  
  甲方负责及时将最新进展反馈给学生，并协调其完成所需操作。  

---

## 6. Stage IV – Offer, Visa and Enrollment  
## 六、阶段四：录取、签证与入学

6.1 **Offer Handling 录取处理**  
- Upon receipt of an offer, Party B reviews key conditions (academic, financial, deadlines) and highlights any critical points to Party A and the Student.  
  乙方在收到录取通知后，审核其中关键条件（学术要求、经济要求、截止日期等），并向甲方及学生提示重点。  

6.2 **Acceptance and Payment 接受录取与缴费**  
- Party A supports the Student to accept the offer within the deadline and to follow the institution’s payment instructions.  
  甲方协助学生在有效期内接受录取，并按照院校要求完成缴费。  
- Party B may, where appropriate, assist in clarifying fee structures or payment plans with the Partner Institution.  
  乙方可视情况协助与合作院校沟通学费结构或缴费计划。  

6.3 **Visa and Pre‑Departure 签证与行前准备**  
- Party B provides high‑level guidance on visa requirements and documentation; Party A and the Student prepare and submit visa applications in accordance with official requirements.  
  乙方就签证要求及材料提供宏观指导；甲方与学生依照官方要求准备并递交签证申请。  
- Both Parties support the Student in understanding pre‑departure arrangements (accommodation, insurance, arrival procedures) within their respective roles.  
  在各自职责范围内，双方协助学生理解并安排入住宿舍、保险、报到流程等行前事项。  

6.4 **Enrollment Confirmation 入学确认**  
- Party A confirms with the Student that enrollment procedures are completed on time.  
  甲方向学生确认其已按时完成报到及注册手续。  
- Party B updates its internal records and, where needed, obtains enrollment confirmation from the Partner Institution.  
  乙方更新内部记录，并在必要时向合作院校获取正式入学确认。  

---

## 7. Stage V – Commission and Settlement  
## 七、阶段五：佣金与结算

7.1 **Upstream Commission 上游佣金到账**  
- Party B monitors commission statements and payments from Partner Institutions, including any conditions applicable to each Student.  
  乙方跟踪合作院校出具的佣金结算单及到账情况，包括适用于每位学生的相关条件。  

7.2 **Rebate to Party A 向甲方返佣**  
- Upon receipt of Base Commission and completion of internal reconciliation, Party B calculates Commission Rebate owed to Party A according to Appendix A (Commission Schedule).  
  在基准佣金到账并完成内部对账后，乙方根据附件一《返佣项目清单》计算应向甲方支付的返佣金额。  
- Party B settles Commission Rebate to Party A within the agreed timeline and provides reasonable statement details upon request.  
  乙方在约定时限内向甲方支付返佣，并在甲方合理要求下提供结算明细。  

7.3 **Student Ledger 学生台账**  
- Party A maintains a ledger covering each Student’s key milestones (lead date, application, offer, enrollment) and expected commission status.  
  甲方维护学生台账，记录线索日期、申请递交、录取情况、入学状态及预期返佣情况。  
- Party B maintains a corresponding internal ledger reflecting Base Commission received and Commission Rebate paid.  
  乙方维护相应的内部台账，记录收到的基准佣金及已支付给甲方的返佣。  

---

## 8. Stage VI – Risk and Special Circumstances  
## 八、阶段六：风险与特殊情形

8.1 **Student Withdrawal / Deferral 学生退学或延期**  
- If a Student withdraws, defers or fails to enroll within the institution’s grace period, Party B may not receive Base Commission or may only receive a reduced amount.  
  如学生在院校规定的宽限期内退学、延期或未完成注册，乙方可能无法收到基准佣金，或仅能收到减少后的佣金。  
- In such cases, Commission Rebate to Party A shall be adjusted accordingly in line with the Referral Commission Cooperation Agreement and Appendix A.  
  在此情形下，乙方向甲方支付的返佣应依《代理合作协议》及附件一相应调整。  

8.2 **Institution Policy Changes 院校政策变更**  
- If Partner Institutions change tuition, commission or enrollment policies, Party B shall inform Party A as soon as reasonably practicable.  
  如合作院校调整学费、佣金或录取政策，乙方应在合理可行的前提下尽快通知甲方。  
- The Parties shall in good faith discuss necessary adjustments to commission arrangements for future Students.  
  双方应本着诚信原则，就未来学生的返佣安排进行必要调整。  

8.3 **Dispute Handling 争议处理**  
- Any disputes or complaints from Students or Partner Institutions should be promptly escalated to Party B, with Party A’s cooperation in fact‑finding and communication.  
  学生或合作院校的任何争议或投诉，应及时上报乙方，并由甲方配合事实调查及沟通。  

---

## 9. Data and Confidentiality  
## 九、数据与保密

9.1 **Data Processing 数据处理**  
- Both Parties shall process Student personal data strictly for purposes related to this cooperation and in compliance with applicable data protection laws (including Singapore’s PDPA).  
  双方仅可为本合作相关目的处理学生个人资料，并遵守适用的数据保护法律（包括新加坡 PDPA）。  
- Data use shall be limited to what is reasonably necessary for counselling, application, enrollment and commission settlement.  
  资料使用应限于为咨询、申请、入学及返佣结算所合理必需的范围。  

9.2 **Confidentiality 保密**  
- Information regarding commission structures, Partner Institutions’ terms and Student applications is confidential and shall not be disclosed to unrelated third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law.  
  关于返佣结构、合作院校条款及学生申请情况的相关信息属于保密信息，除法律要求或经事先书面同意外，不得向无关第三方披露。  

---

## 10. Continuous Improvement 持续优化

10.1 The Parties agree to review this Procedure periodically (for example, annually) in light of market developments, regulatory changes and operational experience, and to refine it where appropriate.  
双方同意结合市场发展、监管变更及运营经验，不定期（如每年）对本流程进行回顾，并在适当情况下进行优化。  

10.2 Any material updates to this Procedure that affect Party A’s daily operations shall be communicated in writing and, where necessary, accompanied by training or briefing sessions.  
对甲方日常操作产生实质影响的流程调整，应以书面形式告知，并在必要时配合培训或说明会。
