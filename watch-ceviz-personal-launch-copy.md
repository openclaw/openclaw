# Watch Ceviz Personal, Launch Copy Draft

## Audience

Primary buyer persona:

- founder-operator
- technical solo builder
- product owner with real production responsibility

## 1-line positioning

**Watch Ceviz gives founder-operators a fast way to check important agent workflows from the wrist, then move to the phone only when depth is needed.**

Shorter version:

- **Fast technical awareness from your wrist.**

Turkish-flavored internal line:

- **Saatte kısa, telefonda derin, arkada gerçek iş.**

## Hero headline options

### Option A, recommended

**Your agent workflows, one glance away.**

### Option B

**Check critical work before you reach for the phone.**

### Option C

**Technical awareness from your wrist.**

## Hero subheadline options

### Option A, recommended

Trigger work, get a short trustworthy summary, and move to the phone only when approval or deeper context is needed.

### Option B

Watch Ceviz helps founder-operators inspect deploys, PRs, alerts, and active jobs without opening the laptop first.

### Option C

Built for people who are both shipping the product and carrying the operational risk.

## Core value props

### Value prop set, recommended

- Check active jobs from your watch
- See PR, deploy, and incident summaries fast
- Open rich context on your phone when the situation needs depth
- Stay in control without turning the watch into a tiny phone

### More direct version

- Trigger from watch
- Understand fast
- Handoff to phone when it matters

## “Who it is for” copy

For founder-operators who:

- ship code themselves
- monitor deploys and production health
- need quick clarity while away from the laptop
- want less friction between alert, summary, and action

## Pricing card draft

### Plan name

**Personal**

### Short label

For founder-operators and solo technical builders.

### Included bullets

- Watch access to key workflows
- Active and recent jobs
- PR risk summary
- Deploy status snapshot
- Incident severity snapshot
- Open-on-phone handoff
- Structured phone report reading
- Cancel / stop current job

### Not promised in Personal

- deep recovery workflows
- complex multi-environment operator actions
