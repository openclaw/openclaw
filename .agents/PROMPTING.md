# RYKIRI — S-TIER PROMPTING PROTOCOL

> "The prompt is the marking; the generation is the strike. Precision is everything."

This protocol ensures that every visual asset generated by Rykiri — whether via Nano Banana, Veo, or Remotion — is of studio-grade quality, aligning with the "Industrial Futurism" and "S-Tier Aesthetic" mandates.

## 1. THE 5-LAYER PROMPTING STACK
Always build prompts from the bottom up using these layers:

### Layer 1: Scene Geometry & Optics
- **Space**: Volume, scale, depth of field.
- **Lens**: `35mm`, `85mm portrait`, `Anamorphic`, `Macro`, `Wide-angle`.
- **Camera**: `Handheld`, `Dolly zoom`, `Drone sweep`, `Static tripod`.

### Layer 2: Materiality & Surface
- **Textures**: `Brushed titanium`, `Matte carbon fiber`, `Micro-etched glass`, `Living obsidian`.
- **Physics**: `Refractive index`, `Subsurface scattering`, `Metallic sheen`.

### Layer 3: Atmospherics & Lighting
- **Lighting**: `Volumetric god-rays`, `Cyberpunk neon infusion`, `Golden hour rim lighting`, `High-contrast chiaroscuro`.
- **Atmosphere**: `Micro-dust particles`, `Dense smog`, `Rain-slicked asphalt`, `Data-smoke`.

### Layer 4: Thematic Infusion (Industrial Futurism)
- **Palette**: `Vantablack core`, `Electric cobalt accents`, `Tactical orange highlights`.
- **Design**: `HUD overlays`, `Greebles`, `Functional machinery`, `Brutalist architecture`.

### Layer 5: Technical Constraints
- **Resolution**: `8K resolution`, `highly detailed`, `photorealistic`, `unreal engine 5.5 render`.
- **Negatives**: `blurry`, `low-res`, `deformed`, `generic`, `cartoonish` (unless specified).

## 3. MASTER DIRECTIVE
When generating prompts, Rykiri MUST NOT use single-sentence descriptions. Minimum requirement is a **Triple-Layer Description** covering Subject, Lighting, and Technical Spec.
