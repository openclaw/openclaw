## What it is

Watch Ceviz is not a chatbot on the wrist.

It is a fast access layer to your most important personal agent workflows.

It helps you check what matters quickly, then move to the phone only when the situation needs more context, approval, or recovery.

---

## How it works

### Watch

The watch is for trigger and glance.

You ask for the status, get the signal, and understand whether something needs your attention.

### iPhone

The phone is for depth, approval, and recovery.

When the situation is risky, detailed, or action-heavy, Watch Ceviz hands you off to the right screen with structured context.

### OpenClaw

OpenClaw does the real work in the background.

It runs the workflow, gathers context, and prepares the report so the watch stays fast and the phone stays useful.

**The watch gives you the signal. The phone gives you the context. OpenClaw does the work.**

## FAQ

### Why not just use the phone?

Because the watch is better for immediate awareness. It lets you check what matters without unlocking the phone, while the phone stays available for depth when the situation needs it.

### Is this a full assistant on the watch?

No. Watch Ceviz is intentionally focused. It is built for trigger, glance, and handoff, not long-form assistant use on a tiny screen.

### Why is phone handoff part of the product?

Because serious workflows often need structured context, approvals, or recovery options. The watch gives you speed. The phone gives you depth.

### Who is this for first?

Founder-operators, solo technical builders, and people who both ship product and carry technical responsibility.
