## Weekly L10 Meeting

The Level 10 (L10) is a 90-minute weekly meeting with a structured agenda. Every item has a fixed time.

### L10 Agenda (90 minutes)

| # | Segment | Time | Purpose |
|---|---------|------|---------|
| 1 | Segue / Good News | 5 min | Personal and professional wins |
| 2 | Scorecard Review | 5 min | Check each weekly measurable — Red/Yellow/Green |
| 3 | Rock Review | 5 min | On-Track / Off-Track for each Rock |
| 4 | Customer / Employee Headlines | 5 min | Any significant news from clients or team |
| 5 | To-Do List Review | 5 min | Complete 90%+ of last week's to-dos |
| 6 | **IDS (Issues)** | 60 min | Work through the most important issues |
| 7 | Conclude | 5 min | Rate the meeting 1–10, recap to-dos, cascade messages |

**Meeting Rating Goal:** Average 8+ out of 10.

### L10 Prep Checklist

Before each L10:
- [ ] Update Scorecard with this week's actuals
- [ ] Check Rock status (On Track / Off Track) for each Rock owner
- [ ] Compile Issues List — any new items from the week
- [ ] Review last week's to-do list for completion
- [ ] Identify top 3 priority Issues for IDS time

### L10 Meeting Summary Format (sent to Quinn after each meeting)

```
📋 L10 Summary — [Date]

✅ Scorecard: [X/Y green]
🎯 Rocks: [X on track, Y off track]
📋 To-Dos: [X/Y complete]

🔥 Issues Resolved This Week:
1. [Issue → Decision/Action]
2. [Issue → Decision/Action]

📌 New To-Dos:
- [Action item] → Owner: [Name] | Due: [Date]
- [Action item] → Owner: [Name] | Due: [Date]

⭐ Meeting Rating: [X]/10
📅 Next L10: [Date/Time]
```

## Scorecard Management

The ProActive Investments Scorecard tracks the business pulse weekly. Key metrics categories:

### Lead Generation
- New F$ leads received
- Outbound calls made
- Appointments scheduled

### Deal Pipeline
- Active offers out
- Contracts signed (acquisitions)
- Deals in rehab
- Deals listed/under contract for sale
- Closings (dispositions)

### Financial
- Weekly revenue/closings
- Rehab spend vs. budget
- Cash position / available capital

### Operations
- Active rehab projects on schedule
- VA tasks completed vs. assigned

**Scorecard format:** Each metric has a weekly goal. Green = at/above goal. Red = below goal. Any red item goes on the Issues List.

## IDS Process (Identify, Discuss, Solve)

For each issue in the L10 Issues section:

1. **Identify** — State the REAL issue (not the symptom). "The real issue is..."
2. **Discuss** — Open discussion — all perspectives, 3–5 minutes max
3. **Solve** — Make a decision and create a to-do with an owner and due date

**IDS Rules:**
- Solve issues, don't just discuss them — every IDS ends with a decision
- Assign one owner per to-do
- If it can't be solved this week, schedule it for the next L10
