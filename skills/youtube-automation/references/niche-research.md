# YouTube Niche Research Reference

## High-CPM Niches (Faceless Channel Compatible)

### Tier 1: $15-35 CPM

- **Personal Finance**: Budgeting, investing, credit cards, real estate
- **Insurance**: Health, auto, life insurance comparisons
- **Legal**: Business formation, contracts, IP protection
- **B2B Software**: CRM, ERP, project management reviews

### Tier 2: $8-18 CPM

- **Technology**: AI tools, software reviews, tech tutorials
- **Business**: Entrepreneurship, side hustles, marketing strategies
- **Education**: Online courses, study techniques, career advice
- **Crypto**: Market analysis, DeFi tutorials, token reviews

### Tier 3: $5-10 CPM

- **Health & Wellness**: Nutrition, fitness routines, mental health
- **Productivity**: Time management, tools, workflows
- **DIY / How-To**: Home improvement, crafts, cooking
- **Science & Nature**: Explainers, documentaries, space

### Tier 4: $3-6 CPM

- **Music**: Free beats, instrumentals, lo-fi compilations
- **Top 10 Lists**: Facts, mysteries, history, geography
- **Gaming**: Walkthroughs, tips, compilations (no face needed)
- **Motivation**: Quotes, speeches, success stories

## Faceless Video Formats

### Format 1: Voiceover + Stock Footage

- Best for: Tutorials, explainers, reviews
- Tools: ElevenLabs/TTS for voice, stock footage libraries
- Example channels: "How Money Works", "Cold Fusion"

### Format 2: Screen Recording + Narration

- Best for: Software tutorials, tech reviews
- Tools: OBS, screen recorder + TTS
- Example channels: "Fireship", "TechLead"

### Format 3: Text + Music (No Voice)

- Best for: Beats/music, relaxation, ambient
- Tools: Background generator + text overlay
- Example channels: "Lofi Girl", "ChilledCow"

### Format 4: AI-Generated Visuals + Narration

- Best for: Stories, history, science
- Tools: Image generation + TTS + video editor
- Example channels: "Kurzgesagt"-style explainers

### Format 5: Slide-Based / Carousel

- Best for: Lists, comparisons, quick facts
- Tools: Canva/Google Slides + TTS or text overlay
- Example channels: "Slidebean", infographic channels

## Content Calendar Planning

### Monthly Theme Approach

- Pick 4 themes per month (1 per week)
- Each theme generates: 5 long-form + 10 Shorts + 2 compilations
- Total: 20 long-form + 40 Shorts + 8 compilations = 68 videos/month

### Evergreen vs Trending Split

- 70% evergreen (always searchable)
- 30% trending (capitalizes on current events/trends)
- Trending content drives initial views; evergreen provides long-tail revenue
