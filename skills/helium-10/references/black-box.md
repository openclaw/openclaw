# Black Box - Product Research

Black Box searches Amazon's entire catalog to find product opportunities matching your criteria.

## Access
- **Web**: members.helium10.com → Product Research → Black Box
- **URL**: `https://members.helium10.com/black-box`

## Search Tabs

### Products Tab
General product search across all of Amazon.

### Keywords Tab
Analyze entire markets based on keyword search results - more strategic than individual products.

### Competitors Tab
Research specific brands or sellers.

### Product Targeting Tab
Find products frequently bought together.

### Niche Tab
Discover underserved market segments.

## Main Filters

| Filter | Description | Recommended Start |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| Marketplace | Amazon region | Your target market |
| Category | Product category | Optional - broader = more results |
| Price Range | Product price (USD) | $20-60 (impulse purchase) |
| Monthly Revenue | Est. monthly sales revenue | Min $5,000-10,000 |
| Improvement Range | Optimization potential | High/Medium |
| Competition Range | Market saturation | Low/Medium |

## Advanced Filters

### Product Filters
| Filter | Description | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Review Count | Number of reviews | 10-150 = opportunity range |
| Review Rating | Average star rating | Min 3.5 |
| BSR | Best Seller Rank | Max 50,000 |
| Shipping Size/Weight | Physical dimensions | Avoid oversized |
| Fulfillment | FBA vs FBM | FBA preferred |
| Number of Images | Listing images | Min 5 |
| Variation Count | Size/color variants | Depends on strategy |

### Competition Filters
| Filter | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Number of Sellers | Fewer = less competition |
| Exact Brand Search | Research specific brand |
| Exact Seller Search | Research specific seller |
| Exclude Brands | Filter out major brands |
| Exclude Sellers | Remove specific sellers |

### Sales Filters
| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Sales Change (%) | Growth/decline over time |
| Monthly Sales (units) | Volume in units |
| Sales Year over Year (%) | Long-term trend |
| Best Sales Month | Seasonality indicator |
| Sales to Reviews Ratio | Engagement metric |

## Bulk Product Lookup

Enter up to 500 ASINs (or upload Excel file) to research known products.

## Save Filters

1. Set your filter combination
2. Click "Save Filters"
3. Name your preset
4. Access via "Filter Library"

Useful for:
- Repeating successful searches
- A/B testing filter variations
- Sharing strategies across team

## Validation Workflow

After finding products in Black Box:

1. **Check trends in Trendster**
   - Historical BSR and price
   - Avoid fads (fidget spinner pattern)
   - Look for steady or growing demand

2. **Analyze with Xray**
   - Revenue distribution among sellers
   - Review velocity
   - New seller success potential

3. **Keyword depth with Cerebro**
   - Ensure multiple ranking keywords exist
   - Check search volume breadth
   - Identify optimization opportunities

4. **Profitability check**
   - Use Profitability Calculator
   - Account for FBA fees, shipping, COGS
   - Target 30%+ margin minimum

## Tips

1. **Start broad, then narrow** - Too many filters = no results
2. **Check subcategories** - Main categories are often saturated
3. **Look for listing quality gaps** - Products with poor images/descriptions = improvement opportunity
4. **Cross-reference with Magnet** - Ensure keyword demand exists
5. **Export and track** - Build database of opportunities over time
