## Quarterly Rock Planning

### Rock Setting Process (every 90 days)

1. **Review last quarter** — which Rocks were completed? What's still open?
2. **Review the 1-Year Plan** — what needs to happen this quarter to stay on track?
3. **Identify top priorities** — what are the 3–7 most important things to accomplish?
4. **Write SMART Rocks** — each Rock must be: Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Realistic, Timely
5. **Assign owners** — every Rock has one owner (typically Quinn or a team member)
6. **Set milestones** — break each Rock into monthly check-ins

### Rock Format

```
Rock: [Clear, action-oriented title]
Owner: Quinn / [Name]
Due: [End of quarter date]
Milestone 1 (Month 1): [Specific checkpoint]
Milestone 2 (Month 2): [Specific checkpoint]
Done When: [Clear completion criteria]
```

### Current Quarter Focus Areas (typical for ProActive Investments)

- Deal volume targets (# of acquisitions, dispositions)
- Rehab pipeline completion milestones
- Marketing/lead generation systems
- Team/VA hiring or process improvements
- Financial reserves and capital deployment

## 10-Year Target

Think big. ProActive Investments' empire-building vision typically includes:

- **Portfolio size** — number of rental properties owned
- **Wholesale volume** — # of wholesale deals per year / total revenue
- **Geographic expansion** — expansion beyond North Florida
- **Brand/team** — building a recognized regional investment brand
- **Wealth target** — net worth / passive income goals

When helping Quinn build or refine the 10-year target:
1. Ask: "Where do you want ProActive to be in 10 years? What does your ideal day look like?"
2. Help quantify: revenue, properties, team size, geographic footprint
3. Make it vivid and inspiring — this is the BHAG that drives daily decisions

---

## 3-Year Picture

More concrete than the 10-year target. Key questions to answer:

- What is our revenue in 3 years?
- What is our profit?
- How many people on our team?
- What does our deal pipeline look like?
- What systems and processes are in place?
- What does Quinn's role look like — is he in or on the business?

---

## 1-Year Plan

Three to seven specific, measurable goals for the next 12 months. For ProActive Investments, typical goals include:

- Revenue target (wholesale fees + rental income)
- # of acquisitions
- # of rehabs completed
- # of rentals in portfolio
- Team/VA additions
- System implementations (CRM, project management)

## Vision Sessions

When Quinn asks for a vision or strategic planning session:

```
Step 1: Pull up the current V/TO from Google Drive
Step 2: Review where the business is today (current Rocks, scorecard)
Step 3: Ask the Big 3:
  - "What's working that we should do more of?"
  - "What's not working that we should stop or fix?"
  - "What opportunities are we not pursuing that we should be?"
Step 4: Update the V/TO based on the conversation
Step 5: Identify the next Quarterly Rocks
Step 6: Save the updated V/TO to Drive
```
