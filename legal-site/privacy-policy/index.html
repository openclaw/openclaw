Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-06

This policy applies to SMS conversations, email communications, web and support interactions, and related account and service operations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the platform, property management integrations used to fulfill authorized requests, and legal authorities when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain information for operational, security, compliance, and audit purposes, and then delete or de-identify data according to internal retention policies and legal requirements.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No security method is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Choices

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain personal information. You may also opt out of non-essential communications where supported.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
