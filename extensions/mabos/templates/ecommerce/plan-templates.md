# E-Commerce Plan Templates

## PT-EC-001: Product Launch

**Applicable Goals:** Launch new product, SKU expansion
**Success Rate:** 74%

### Steps

1. Market research — demand validation, competitor pricing (compound)
2. Product sourcing/creation — vendor selection, samples (compound)
3. Listing creation — copy, images, SEO (primitive)
4. Inventory planning — initial order quantity, reorder point (primitive)
5. Pricing strategy — margin analysis, competitive positioning (primitive)
6. Marketing campaign — launch ads, email blast, social (compound)
7. Monitor first 48h — sales velocity, conversion rate (primitive)
8. Iterate — adjust pricing, ads, copy based on data (primitive)

---

## PT-EC-002: Inventory Optimization

**Applicable Goals:** Reduce stockouts, minimize carrying costs
**Success Rate:** 81%

### Steps

1. Pull sales velocity data by SKU (primitive)
2. Calculate reorder points and safety stock (primitive)
3. Identify slow movers and dead stock (primitive)
4. Generate liquidation plan for dead stock (primitive)
5. Adjust reorder quantities based on seasonality (primitive)
6. Set up automated reorder alerts (primitive)

---

## PT-EC-003: Customer Acquisition Campaign

**Applicable Goals:** Increase traffic, reduce CAC, grow revenue
**Success Rate:** 68%

### Steps

1. Define target audience and channels (primitive)
2. Set budget allocation across channels (primitive → decision_request if > threshold)
3. Create ad creative and landing pages (compound)
4. Launch campaigns — paid search, social, email (compound)
5. Monitor daily — CPA, ROAS, conversion rate (primitive)
6. Optimize — pause underperformers, scale winners (primitive)
7. Weekly report to CMO and CFO (primitive)

## PT-EC-004: Fulfillment Process Setup

**Applicable Goals:** Establish shipping, returns, customer service
**Success Rate:** 86%

### Steps

1. Select fulfillment model — self, 3PL, hybrid (decision point)
2. Set up shipping carriers and rates (primitive)
3. Define returns policy and process (primitive)
4. Create customer service playbooks (primitive)
5. Test end-to-end order flow (primitive)
6. Document SOPs for COO (primitive)
