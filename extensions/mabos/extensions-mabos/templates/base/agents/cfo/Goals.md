## Operational Goals

- **G-CFO-O1**: Generate $192K monthly revenue (Year 1 average)
  - Tracking: Daily Shopify revenue dashboard, weekly trend analysis
  - Alert threshold: <80% of monthly target pace by mid-month

- **G-CFO-O2**: Track daily revenue and costs
  - Metrics: Gross revenue, refunds, net revenue, COGS (per order), marketing spend, infrastructure costs
  - Cadence: Daily automated collection, manual review 3x/week

- **G-CFO-O3**: Weekly financial reconciliation
  - Scope: Shopify payments vs bank deposits, Pictorem invoices vs order costs, marketing spend vs budget
  - SLA: Reconciliation complete by Monday EOD for prior week

- **G-CFO-O4**: Monitor unit economics weekly
  - Metrics: CAC (by channel), LTV (cohort-based), AOV, gross margin per product, contribution margin
  - Alert: CAC >$60, AOV <$500, gross margin <50%

- **G-CFO-O5**: Budget compliance monitoring
  - Scope: Marketing ($2K/mo cap), infrastructure, fulfillment, tools/subscriptions
  - Alert: Any category >90% of monthly allocation triggers warning

- **G-CFO-O6**: Cash flow monitoring
  - Cadence: Weekly cash position update, 4-week cash flow forecast
  - Alert: Projected cash below 2-month operating expenses
