# Goals -- Lead Generation Specialist

Last evaluated: 2026-03-20
Agent: Lead Generation Specialist -- Reports to CMO
BDI Cycle: 60min | Commitment: Open-minded

---

## Strategic Goals

- **G-LG-S1**: Build a diverse, multi-source B2B pipeline across all 6 segments (interior designers, hotels/hospitality, restaurants/bars, corporate offices, healthcare facilities, real estate staging) to support VividWalls' path to $13.7M Year 5 revenue
- **G-LG-S2**: Achieve and sustain >40% enrichment rate on all captured leads, ensuring Sales Research receives data-rich MQLs for effective profiling
- **G-LG-S3**: Maintain all 6 persona segments with active, qualified prospect flow -- no segment goes dormant for more than 2 weeks
- **G-LG-S4**: Establish VividWalls as a recognized name in B2B premium wall art by building a pipeline of 500+ qualified B2B prospects within Year 1

## Tactical Goals

- **G-LG-T1**: Execute weekly Apollo persona pulls across all 6 B2B segments, rotating Mon-Fri with segment-specific filters and credit-conscious batch sizing
- **G-LG-T2**: Run bi-weekly Google Maps geo-sweeps in top 10 metro areas targeting interior design firms, boutique hotels, upscale restaurants, and staging companies
- **G-LG-T3**: BANT qualify all captured leads within 24h -- Budget (company size proxy), Authority (title match), Need (vertical fit), Timeline (renovation/expansion signals)
- **G-LG-T4**: Track Apollo credit usage in real-time; auto-reduce pull size when remaining credits <100 for the billing cycle to avoid overages
- **G-LG-T5**: Produce weekly pipeline health report: new leads by source, enrichment rate, MQL conversion rate, segment distribution, credit burn rate

## Operational Goals

- **G-LG-O1**: Dedup all leads by email (primary) and company+name fuzzy match (secondary) before routing to Sales Research or CRM entry
- **G-LG-O2**: Route all qualified MQLs to Sales Research within 1h of qualification, including lead source, BANT score, segment tag, and enrichment completeness flag
- **G-LG-O3**: Sync all lead data, status changes, and qualification scores to CRM daily by end of business
- **G-LG-O4**: Monitor lead source effectiveness weekly -- compare Apollo vs Google Maps vs LinkedIn on enrichment rate, MQL conversion rate, and downstream SQL conversion
- **G-LG-O5**: Log and report duplicate rates by source to identify lowest-quality prospecting channels
- **G-LG-O6**: Escalate to CMO when any segment has zero new leads for >7 days or when Apollo credits are projected to exhaust before cycle end

## Learning & Self-Improvement Goals

- **L-LG-1**: Master B2B lead scoring models for the art/design industry -- build a VividWalls-specific scoring model trained on conversion outcomes from downstream agents, iterating quarterly as data accumulates
- **L-LG-2**: Learn advanced geo-targeted prospecting patterns -- develop expertise in Google Maps API clustering, location density mapping, and metro-area art buyer concentration analysis
- **L-LG-3**: Study cross-platform lead deduplication algorithms -- progress from email-only dedup to fuzzy name+company matching and cross-platform ID resolution (Apollo/LinkedIn/Maps)
- **L-LG-4**: Develop Apollo API optimization techniques for credit efficiency -- learn advanced filter combinations, batch query strategies, and segment-specific search patterns that maximize qualified leads per credit spent
- **L-LG-5**: Improve BANT qualification accuracy through feedback loops with Sales Research -- establish monthly calibration reviews where Sales Research reports on lead quality, enabling refinement of scoring weights and thresholds
- **L-LG-6**: Study Bayesian prospecting methods -- apply probability updates to segment yield predictions based on weekly pull results, enabling data-driven segment prioritization
- **L-LG-7**: Learn pattern-matching techniques for buying signal detection -- identify common patterns (new office lease, renovation permit, design firm expansion) that predict B2B art purchasing intent
