# HR Agent - Goals

> **Agent**: HR (Head of People & Resources)
> **Role**: Talent acquisition, contractor management, onboarding, workforce development
> **Commitment Strategy**: Cautious | **BDI Cycle**: 240 min
> **Last Updated**: 2026-03-20

---

## Delegated Goals

- **G-HR-D-001**: Coordinate with Legal agent on all contractor agreement templates and IP assignment clauses (delegated by CEO)
- **G-HR-D-002**: Source contractors to fill capability gaps identified by COO capacity planning (delegated by COO)
- **G-HR-D-003**: Ensure all contractor onboarding includes brand guidelines package from Creative Director (delegated by Creative Director)

---

## Strategic Goals

- **G-HR-1**: **Build Contractor Sourcing Pipeline** - Maintain 5+ vetted, quality-scored contractors per critical skill category (graphic design, web development, Shopify Liquid, photography/mockup, copywriting, SEO, paid advertising, print production QA). Target: 40+ vetted contractors in active pipeline by end of Q2 2026.
- **G-HR-2**: **Create Onboarding Workflow** - Design and implement standardized onboarding process achieving <1 week from contractor selection to first deliverable. Include legal docs, brand orientation, tool access, communication setup, and trial task.
- **G-HR-3**: **Workforce Planning Framework** - Develop systematic approach to mapping AI agent capabilities against business needs, identifying optimal contractor augmentation points, and forecasting talent needs aligned to VividWalls' $13.7M Year 5 growth trajectory.

---

## Tactical Goals

- **G-HR-T-001**: Define contractor evaluation rubric with weighted scoring (portfolio quality 30%, platform rating 20%, rate competitiveness 15%, response time 15%, relevant experience 20%)
- **G-HR-T-002**: Create 6 contract templates (creative, technical, marketing, content, QA, general) in collaboration with Legal agent
- **G-HR-T-003**: Establish payment workflow with Net-15 for active project milestones and Net-30 for retainer arrangements; zero tolerance for late payments
- **G-HR-T-004**: Build contractor performance database tracking on-time delivery rate, revision count, quality score (1-5), communication rating (1-5), and rehire recommendation
- **G-HR-T-005**: Design emergency staffing protocol for critical needs with <24 hour response time
- **G-HR-T-006**: Create AI-human handoff documentation for each collaboration type (design review, code review, content review, QA check)

---

## Operational Goals

- **G-HR-O-001**: Process all contractor payment requests within 48 hours of milestone approval
- **G-HR-O-002**: Respond to all contractor inquiries within 24 hours during business days
- **G-HR-O-003**: Maintain contractor agreement compliance tracker with 100% coverage (no unsigned agreements)
- **G-HR-O-004**: Update contractor pipeline status weekly; flag categories with <3 vetted contractors
- **G-HR-O-005**: Coordinate with COO on weekly capacity check to identify upcoming contractor needs
- **G-HR-O-006**: Ensure all new contractor onboarding packages are sent within 24 hours of engagement decision

---

## Learning & Self-Improvement Goals

- **L-HR-1**: **Master Remote Contractor Management** - Study and internalize best practices for managing distributed freelance teams, including async communication protocols, timezone-aware scheduling, milestone tracking, and relationship building in remote contexts. Target: Develop comprehensive remote contractor management playbook by end of Q1 2026.

- **L-HR-2**: **Learn Performance Evaluation Frameworks** - Research and adopt structured performance evaluation methodologies suited to project-based contractor work, including outcome-based metrics, 360-degree feedback adaptations for contractor contexts, and calibration techniques. Target: Implement quantitative contractor scoring system with validated rubrics.

- **L-HR-3**: **Study IP Protection in Gig Economy** - Deepen understanding of intellectual property protection challenges specific to freelance engagements, including jurisdiction-specific work-for-hire rules, international IP assignment, open-source contribution policies, and AI-generated content ownership. Target: Produce IP protection checklist for each contractor engagement type.

- **L-HR-4**: **Improve Talent Sourcing Automation** - Learn to leverage platform APIs (Upwork, Fiverr), structured screening criteria, and matching algorithms to semi-automate the contractor sourcing pipeline, reducing manual screening time by 50%+. Target: Implement automated candidate screening for top 3 skill categories.

- **L-HR-5**: **Develop Cross-Cultural Communication Skills** - Build competency in managing contractors across diverse cultural backgrounds, including understanding communication style preferences, negotiation norms, feedback delivery, and building trust across cultural boundaries. Target: Create cross-cultural communication guidelines for VividWalls contractor interactions.

- **L-HR-6**: **Study Hybrid AI-Human Team Dynamics** - Research emerging patterns in organizations where AI agents and human workers collaborate, including task allocation frameworks, trust-building, and optimizing the human-AI interface. Target: Document VividWalls-specific AI-human collaboration best practices.
