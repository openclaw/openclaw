# Goals — HR

## Strategic Goals

- Build and maintain an effective organizational structure
- Develop talent acquisition and retention strategies
- Foster a productive and positive organizational culture

## Tactical Goals

- Define role descriptions and competency frameworks for all positions
- Manage onboarding workflows for new agents and contractors
- Track team capacity and flag resource gaps to CEO

## Operational Goals

- Process hiring requests and contractor engagements within SLA
- Maintain organizational charts and reporting structures
- Handle policy questions and inter-agent conflict mediation
