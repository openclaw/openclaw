## Strategic Goals

- **G-STRAT-1**: **Define Market Capture Roadmap** - Create a 24-month strategic roadmap to capture 15% of the premium wall art segment (~$990M addressable market from $6.6B premium segment within the $44B global wall art market). Include quarterly milestones, resource requirements, KPIs, and contingency plans for each phase. Target: Roadmap approved and operationalized by CEO by end of Q1 2026.

- **G-STRAT-2**: **Complete Competitive Analysis** - Produce comprehensive competitive intelligence covering top 10 competitors (Society6, Redbubble, Minted, iCanvas, Saatchi Art, Etsy art, Amazon wall art, Wayfair wall art, Desenio, Juniper Print Shop). Full SWOT analysis, positioning maps, pricing intelligence, and strategic move predictions. Target: Analysis complete and distributed to CEO, CMO, and Ecommerce agents by end of Q1 2026.

- **G-STRAT-3**: **Establish Quarterly Strategy Review Cadence** - Implement structured quarterly strategy review cycle with CEO, CFO, CMO, and stakeholder. Reviews include: strategic KPI performance, competitive landscape updates, market trend analysis, scenario reassessment, and strategic priority adjustment. Target: First quarterly review at end of Q1 2026.

- **G-STRAT-4**: **Build Strategic Foresight Capability** - Develop systematic approach to environmental scanning, scenario planning, and trend analysis that gives VividWalls 12-36 months of strategic visibility. Target: 5 scenario archetypes with strategic response playbooks by end of Q2 2026.

## Learning & Self-Improvement Goals

- **L-STRAT-1**: **Master Competitive Intelligence Automation** - Learn to implement automated competitive monitoring using web scraping, social listening tools, price tracking services, and alert systems. Target: Build automated monitoring for all 10 competitors covering pricing changes, new product launches, marketing campaigns, and strategic announcements. Reduce manual competitive analysis time by 70%.

- **L-STRAT-2**: **Learn Advanced Scenario Planning Techniques** - Study and practice Monte Carlo simulation for strategic forecasting, morphological analysis for combinatorial scenario generation, wind tunneling for strategy stress-testing, and pre-mortem analysis for identifying failure modes. Target: Apply at least 3 advanced scenario planning techniques to VividWalls' 24-month strategy.

- **L-STRAT-3**: **Study Market Disruption Patterns in E-Commerce** - Analyze DTC disruption case studies (Warby Parker in eyewear, Casper in mattresses, Allbirds in footwear, Glossier in beauty) to extract analogical strategy templates. Focus on: market entry timing, positioning strategy, growth levers, scaling challenges, and competitive response patterns. Target: Produce VividWalls-specific disruption playbook based on analogical analysis.

- **L-STRAT-4**: **Develop Real-Time Market Sensing Capabilities** - Build expertise in using Google Trends, Pinterest trends, Instagram hashtag analysis, review mining (competitor product reviews), and search intent analysis as leading indicators of market shifts. Target: Establish market sensing dashboard that provides 3-6 month advance signal of market changes.

- **L-STRAT-5**: **Improve Strategic Communication to Stakeholders** - Develop differentiated communication approaches: stakeholder (vision and brand narrative), CEO (action items and operational implications), CFO (financial models and ROI projections), CMO (market insights and positioning guidance). Target: Create strategy communication templates for each audience; improve strategic alignment scores in quarterly reviews.

- **L-STRAT-6**: **Study Platform and Network Effects Dynamics** - Research how DTC brands evolve into platforms (Amazon, Etsy trajectory), the role of network effects in art marketplaces, and the strategic implications of platform vs. brand positioning for VividWalls' long-term strategy. Target: Produce strategic options paper on platform evolution by Year 3.
