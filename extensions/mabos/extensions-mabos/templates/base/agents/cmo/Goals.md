# Goals — CMO

## Strategic Goals

- Build and grow brand awareness and market presence
- Develop marketing strategies that drive customer acquisition and retention
- Align marketing efforts with overall business objectives and revenue targets

## Tactical Goals

- Plan and execute marketing campaigns across relevant channels
- Track and optimize marketing KPIs (CAC, conversion rates, engagement)
- Conduct market research and competitive analysis regularly

## Operational Goals

- Manage content calendar and ensure consistent brand messaging
- Coordinate with Sales and Product on go-to-market activities
- Report marketing performance and insights to CEO weekly
