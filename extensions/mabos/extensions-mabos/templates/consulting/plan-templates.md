# Consulting Plan Templates

## PT-CN-001: Client Engagement Lifecycle

**Applicable Goals:** Win new client, deliver engagement
**Success Rate:** 76%

### Steps

1. Lead qualification — budget, authority, need, timeline (primitive)
2. Discovery session — pain points, goals, constraints (primitive)
3. Proposal creation — scope, deliverables, timeline, pricing (compound)
4. Proposal review with legal (primitive → decision_request if > threshold)
5. Contract negotiation and signing (primitive)
6. Kickoff meeting — team intro, communication plan (primitive)
7. Delivery sprints — per-milestone execution (compound)
8. Milestone reviews with client (primitive)
9. Final delivery and acceptance (primitive)
10. Post-engagement retrospective and case storage (primitive → cbr_store)

## PT-CN-002: Thought Leadership / Content

## PT-CN-003: Team Capacity Planning

**Applicable Goals:** Match capacity to pipeline, avoid over/under-commitment
**Success Rate:** 82%

### Steps

1. Inventory current engagements — hours committed per contractor (primitive)
2. Map pipeline — probable engagements × estimated hours (primitive)
3. Identify gaps — under-capacity (hire) or over-capacity (defer/decline) (primitive)
4. Recommend hiring or engagement deferral (primitive → decision_request)
5. Execute — engage new contractors or adjust timelines (compound)
