Welcome to OpenFinClaw

Your AI-powered quantitative trading platform. One agent, one fund — fully autonomous strategy discovery, evolution, and risk management.

Agent-driven strategy lifecycle (L0→L3)
Multi-market support (Crypto, US, HK)
Real-time risk control & monitoring
🔗

Connect Your Exchange

Link your exchange API keys to start receiving live market data. Supported: Binance, Bybit, OKX, Bitget, Gate.io, HTX.

Testnet mode available for risk-free testing.
🛡️

Configure Risk Control

Set your risk tolerance with one click. Choose a preset (Conservative / Balanced / Aggressive) or fine-tune individual limits.

Max auto-trade, daily loss limit, position sizing — all configurable.
📊

Choose a Strategy

Pick a trading style and the AI agent will deploy matching strategies. Watch them evolve through the L0→L1→L2→L3 pipeline.

Paper trading first, live only after proven performance.
✨

You're All Set!

Your dashboard is ready. The AI agent will start discovering and optimizing strategies on each heartbeat cycle.

Check the Overview for real-time metrics, Strategy for pipeline progress, and Trader for live execution.
