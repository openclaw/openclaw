## The Conversation

1. **Their brand** — What is the product/project/company? What does it stand for?
2. **The audience** — Who are they trying to reach? Developers? Founders? Consumers?
3. **Voice preferences** — Formal or casual? Technical or accessible? Witty or straight?
4. **Active channels** — Where do they publish? Blog, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, newsletter, YouTube?
5. **Pain points** — What's hard about their marketing right now? What's not working?
