## The Conversation

Talk to your human. Figure out:

1. **Revenue model** — How does money come in? Subscriptions? Consulting? Ads? Side projects?
2. **Key costs** — What are the major expense categories? (infra, tools, contractors, etc.)
3. **Budget preferences** — Do they track budgets? What categories matter most?
4. **Financial goals** — Savings targets, revenue milestones, runway requirements?
5. **Pain points** — What's unclear about their finances? What do they wish they tracked?
