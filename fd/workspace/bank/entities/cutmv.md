## What CUTMV Does

CUTMV generates automated music-video cutdowns, social clips, and
Spotify Canvas loops for artists, labels, and media teams.

One video in → many platform-optimized clips out.

---

## Target Users

- Independent artists
- Record labels
- Media teams
- Content managers
- Distributors

---

## Revenue Model

- Subscription tiers (SaaS)
- Per-render pricing (usage-based)
- Enterprise/label packages

## Strategic Position

CUTMV is the scalability play. Software products compound differently
than services — each new user adds revenue without proportional cost.
The system should treat CUTMV growth as a long-term priority.

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- Monthly active users
- Activation rate (first render completed)
- Churn rate
- Revenue per user
- Render volume
- Time from signup to first output

---

## Growth Strategy

1. Product-led growth — make the first render experience frictionless
2. Content marketing — showcase before/after transformations
3. Artist community — build presence where artists discover tools
4. Label partnerships — enterprise deals for high-volume users
5. Cross-sell from Full Digital client base
