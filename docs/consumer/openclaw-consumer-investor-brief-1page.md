# OpenClaw Consumer Wrapper — 1-Page Investor Brief

## Vision

A personal AI operator that does real digital work for users from Telegram, running on their own Mac.

## Problem

Most AI assistants are strong at chat but weak at execution. Users still manually perform real tasks across browsers, files, apps, and accounts.

## Solution

A local-first execution product with a simple chat interface:

- Telegram as command surface
- Local runtime on the user’s Mac for high capability and session continuity
- Safety profiles plus confirmation only for irreversible actions
- Transparent activity timeline + panic pause

## Why Now

- User behavior is shifting from “ask AI” to “delegate work”
- Computer-use agents are proving demand
- Trust and reliability are better when execution happens on user-owned infrastructure

## Product Scope (MVP)

1. macOS installer + guided onboarding
2. Telegram-first setup
3. Safety profiles (Safe/Balanced/Power)
4. Irreversible-action confirmation gate (send/payment/delete/security)
5. Timeline and panic pause
6. Runtime health + auto-reconnect
7. Billing + licensing

## GTM Strategy

- Founder-led onboarding with high-intent power users
- Fast iteration in private beta before broad launch
- Public proof via real outcomes (“agent completed X”) and weekly shipping cadence

## Business Model

- Starter (Local): $29/mo
- Pro (Local+): $79/mo
- Concierge (Managed Mac): $249–$499/mo

## Moat

- Reliability on real workflows (not demo tasks)
- Trust UX (auditable actions, controllable autonomy)
- Tight integration between chat orchestration and local execution

## 90-Day Milestones

1. Launch MVP and onboard first 100 paying users
2. Hit activation and retention thresholds
3. Validate managed-host premium tier with pilot customers

## KPI Targets

- Time to first value: <10 minutes
- Week-1 retained users: strong active delegation behavior
- Autonomy success rate: high completion with low manual rescue
- Trust incidents: near zero critical side-effects
