# Qenjin Brand Strategy — Canonical Reference

**Source:** IxH OSINT research session
**Status:** Validated positioning. Use as ground truth for competitive intel, messaging alignment, and outreach copy.

---

## Brand Identity

**Tagline:** "Organize your signal. Secure your source."
**Voice:** Calm authority. Ethical, precise, no hype. Neuroscience lab meets ops dashboard.
**Visual:** Deep navy, muted teal, clean sans-serif, subtle neural network motifs.
**Moat:** No one combines AI-guided UX + Whonix-style security + multi-use-case ops under one brand.

## Three-Product Portfolio

### Product 1: Open Bodhi — Private AI Engine

- **Role:** Foundational infrastructure. Powers everything. Sells standalone.
- **Target:** Developers, self-hosters, privacy obsessives, wellness practitioners, biohackers.
- **Core promise:** Third brain partner. Local or self-hosted. Your rawest data never leaks.
- **Pricing:** Freemium self-host (local LLMs free), Bedrock tier $29/mo, Enterprise $99/mo/user.
- **Key functions:** First-boot voice setup, thought clustering, body analysis (local models), personalized reasoning loops, Whonix-style isolation, OpenAI-compatible API.

### Product 2: Qenjin Console — Operations UI/UX Layer

- **Role:** Customer-facing magic dashboard. Built on Open Bodhi.
- **Target:** Small businesses (0-50 staff) — clinics, warehouses, creators, agencies.
- **Core promise:** Pick use case on boot, get AI walkthrough, organized in 3-5 mins.
- **Pricing:** $49/mo base (1 use case), $99/mo unlimited + custom.
- **Use cases (8+ predefined):**
  - Ketamine Clinics — PHQ-9 tracking, session summaries, outcome funnels
  - Warehouse Inventory — reorder forecasting, waste detection, layout optimization
  - Writer Thoughts Organizer — idea clustering, Substack pipeline, theme graphs
  - Agency Projects — client pipelines, task diffusion, status reports
  - Ecom Fulfillment — order flows, delay prediction, upsells
  - Content Calendar — scheduling, performance retro, AI topic suggestions
  - Clinic Patient Success (general) — any wellness cohort tracking
  - Personal Knowledge Base — Obsidian-style, AI-curated
  - Custom Build — AI voice interviews you, generates bespoke dashboard

### Product 3: Qenjin Webbing — Marketing Automation Flagship

- **Role:** Growth engine, main revenue driver. Ties Console to acquisition.
- **Target:** Same small businesses + solopreneurs/creators.
- **Core promise:** Hands-off marketing using operations data from Console/Bodhi.
- **Pricing:** $199/mo starter, $499/mo pro (unlimited channels + agent).
- **Key functions:** Funnel builder (voice-guided), lead scoring from Console data, multi-channel automation (email/SMS/LinkedIn/YouTube), content repurposing, A/B testing, HIPAA-aware compliance layer, self-running Webbing Agent.

## Go-to-Market SOP

### Pre-launch (Weeks 1-2)
- Build qenjin.com: "Three products. One private AI core."
- Use-case carousel on home. Demo videos (Loom).
- Lead magnet: "Pick your use case — get free AI setup guide."

### Phase 1: Founder-led validation (Weeks 3-6)
- Personal Qenjin agent generates outreach.
- Target ketamine clinics (LinkedIn/Reddit). 20 emails/day.
- Offer Console demo. Convert to paid pilots ($500/mo).

### Phase 2: Content flywheel (Months 2-3)
- Substack: "Ketamine insights without patient AI."
- Repurpose via Webbing. YouTube lives: "Build your third brain."
- Target: 500 signups.

### Phase 3: Product-led growth (Months 4+)
- Freemium Bodhi hooks devs.
- Console trials (14 days). Webbing upsell.
- Affiliate program for clinic operators.

### Cross-product loops
- Console → Webbing: "Automate your clinic growth"
- Bodhi → Console: "Unlock business use cases"

## Competitive Differentiation
- Generic SaaS stacks: leaky, no AI voice setup, no privacy
- Consumer AIs (ChatGPT, Claude): cloud-only, no local ops, no compliance
- Notion/Obsidian: no AI automation, no business ops, no marketing layer
- Qenjin: the only stack that is local-first + AI-guided + multi-use-case + marketing-native
