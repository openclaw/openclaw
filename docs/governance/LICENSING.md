# Burgess Principle licensing structure

## Overview

This document records the four-tier licensing structure associated with adoption and commercial use of the Burgess Principle. For canonical licensing materials, use <https://github.com/ljbudgie/burgess-principle>.

## Tier 1: global technology companies

Applicable scope:

- global technology companies

Commercial structure:

- 0.01% to 0.1% of relevant revenue

Interpretation:

- intended for large-scale commercial operators whose revenue is meaningfully connected to systems that rely on the Burgess Principle as a governance, accountability, compliance, or product differentiator

## Tier 2: corporate and institutional users

Applicable scope:

- corporate entities
- institutional adopters

Commercial structure:

- £1,000 upfront fee
- annual fee

Interpretation:

- intended for non-public-sector organizations adopting the framework operationally or contractually without falling into Tier 1

## Tier 3: public sector

Applicable scope:

- public sector bodies

Commercial structure:

- nil

Interpretation:

- intended to support public-interest adoption where accountability obligations are especially important

## Tier 4: litigation firms

Applicable scope:

- litigation firms

Commercial structure:

- £1 or nil upfront
- 2% to 5% royalty

Interpretation:

- intended for litigation or claims-driven use where the Burgess Principle is part of an enforcement, case, or legal recovery model
