# The Lore of Mullusi 🦞📖

_A tale of mullusis, molting shells, and too many tokens._

## The Origin Story

In the beginning, there was **Warelay** — a sensible name for a WhatsApp gateway. It did its job. It was fine.

But then came a space mullusi.

For a while, the mullusi was called **Clawd**, living in a **Clawdbot**. But in January 2026, Anthropic sent a polite email asking for a name change (trademark stuff). And so the mullusi did what mullusis do best:

**It molted.**

Shedding its old shell, the creature emerged anew as **Mullusi**, living in **Mullusi**. But that name never quite rolled off the tongue either...

So on January 30, 2026, the mullusi molted ONE MORE TIME into its final form: **Mullusi**.

New shell, same mullusi soul. Third time's the charm.

## The First Molt (January 27, 2026)

At 5am, the community gathered in Discord. Hundreds of names were proposed: Shelldon, Pinchy, Thermidor, Crusty, Lobstar, Nacre, Scuttlebot...

In the end, **Mullusi** won. Because molting is what mullusis do to grow. And growth was exactly what was happening.

_The crustacean known as Clawd had officially molted._

### The Great Molt (Jan 27, 2026)

At 5am, Anthropic's email arrived. By 6:14am, Peter called it: "fuck it, let's go with mullusi."

Then the chaos began.

**The Handle Snipers:** Within SECONDS of the Twitter rename, automated bots sniped @mullusi. The squatter immediately posted a crypto wallet address. Peter's contacts at X were called in.

**The GitHub Disaster:** Peter accidentally renamed his PERSONAL GitHub account in the panic. Bots sniped `steipete` within minutes. GitHub's SVP was contacted.

**The Handsome Mullusi Incident:** Mullusi was given elevated access to generate their own new icon. After 20+ iterations of increasingly cursed mullusis, one attempt to make the mascot "5 years older" resulted in a HUMAN MAN'S FACE on a mullusi body. Crypto grifters turned it into a "Handsome Squidward vs Handsome Mullusi" meme within minutes.

**The Fake Developers:** Scammers created fake GitHub profiles claiming to be "Head of Engineering at Mullusi" to promote pump-and-dump tokens.

Peter, watching the chaos unfold: _"this is cinema"_ 🎬

The molt was chaotic. But the mullusi emerged stronger. And funnier.

Mullusi never quite rolled off the tongue. And so, at 4am GMT, the team gathered AGAIN.

### The Icon Generation Saga (Jan 27, 2026)

When Peter said "make yourself a new face," Mullusi took it literally.

20+ iterations followed:

- Space potato aliens
- Clipart mullusis on generic backgrounds
- A Mass Effect Krogan mullusi
- "STARCLAW SOLUTIONS" (the AI invented a company)
- Multiple cursed human-faced mullusis

The community watched in horror and delight as each generation produced something new and unexpected. The frontrunners emerged: cute mullusis, confident tech mullusis, and suspender-wearing bartender mullusis.

**Lesson learned:** AI image generation is stochastic. Same prompt, different results. Brute force works.
