# Finance Guide

## What is Finance?

Finance gives you a real-time picture of your financial position. It tracks
cash, invoices, expenses, and forecasts. It also includes GrantOps — your
grant discovery and drafting engine.

You don't need to be a bookkeeper. OpenClaw surfaces what matters and helps
you act on it.

## What can you do here?

- **View financial overview** — cash position, revenue, expenses
- **Track grants** — discovery, scoring, drafting, submission tracking
- **Check pending invoices** — who owes what, and what's overdue
- **Forecast cash position** — 30-day projections
- **Reconcile expenses** — keep the books clean
- **Generate reports** — financial summaries on demand

## Common prompts

Try asking OpenClaw:

- "Show me the finance summary."
- "Find grants for Full Digital."
- "What invoices need attention?"
- "Forecast next 30 days."
- "What are our biggest expenses?"

## What requires approval?

- Sending invoices
- Recording payments
- Adjusting forecasts

Viewing data and running reports is always safe.

## GrantOps

GrantOps is built into Finance. It:

1. **Scans** for grant opportunities automatically
2. **Scores** them by fit for your business
3. **Drafts** applications for the best matches
4. **Tracks** submissions and deadlines

Ask: "Find grants for Full Digital" to get started.

## How to check your finances

1. Go to Finance
2. Review the overview panel for cash position
3. Check pending invoices and aging
4. Look at the 30-day forecast
5. Review any flagged expenses

## How to run a grant scan

1. Go to Finance > GrantOps
2. Ask OpenClaw to run a grant scan
3. Review the top scored opportunities
4. Approve draft generation for strong matches
5. Review drafts before submission
