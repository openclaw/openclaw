# Marketing Guide

## What is Marketing?

Marketing helps you spend smarter. It runs campaign analysis, generates ad
concepts, suggests what to test next, and prepares safe scaling actions.

OpenClaw will never spend money without your approval. It analyzes, recommends,
and waits for your green light.

## What can you do here?

- **Analyze ad performance** — see what's working and what's not
- **Generate ad concepts** — hooks, angles, copy variations
- **Suggest next tests** — data-driven testing recommendations
- **Prepare scaling plans** — safe, capped budget increases
- **Rotate creatives** — keep ads fresh
- **Propose campaign changes** — optimization recommendations

## Common prompts

Try asking OpenClaw:

- "Generate 3 CUTMV ad concepts."
- "Why are ads underperforming?"
- "Scale winners carefully."
- "What should we test next?"
- "Show me this week's performance."
- "Which campaigns are winning?"

## What requires approval?

- Launching campaigns
- Increasing budget
- Applying high-risk changes

Generating concepts, analyzing performance, and viewing reports is always safe.

## How to scale ads safely

1. Go to Marketing
2. Ask OpenClaw to analyze campaign performance
3. Review the recommended scaling actions
4. Check spend impact, cap usage, and projected totals
5. If safe, approve the scaling action
6. If blocked, review the reallocation or alternative plan

OpenClaw will warn you before you exceed any caps.

## How to generate ad concepts

1. Go to Marketing
2. Ask: "Generate 3 CUTMV ad concepts" (or Full Digital)
3. Review the generated hooks, angles, and copy
4. Pick what you like
5. Request full creative briefs for your favorites
6. Approve for production or request revisions
