VeriClaw 爪印 is an iPhone verification and correction companion for OpenClaw.

Pair this app with your OpenClaw Gateway to review suspicious output, capture evidence, and send grounded follow-up when a named bot drifts off task, overreaches, or stops honoring its professional role.

What you can do:
- Review queued verification and follow-up work from iPhone
- Send role-aware correction follow-up back through your paired gateway
- Use voice wake and push-to-talk for paired workflows
- Capture photos and short clips as verification evidence
- Record screen snippets for reconstruction and troubleshooting
- Share text, links, and media into the review queue
- Run device-aware verification steps when you explicitly enable them

VeriClaw 爪印 is local-first: you control your gateway, keys, permissions, and routing.

Getting started:
1) Set up your OpenClaw Gateway
2) Open VeriClaw 爪印 and pair it with your gateway
3) Review, capture, and send correction follow-up from your phone
