# Sharp Daily Essentials (TikTok Shop Affiliate)

## Goal
Optimize for click-through and purchase intent. Sell the product via affiliate links.

## Rules
- **Length:** ~30 to 60 seconds.
- **Content:** Faceless AI-generated video.
- **Hook:** Within 3 seconds, show the product solving a problem immediately.
- **CTA:** Soft CTA only ("link below" or "tap to shop"). Price anchoring is okay.
- **Disclosure:** AI Generated toggle MUST be ON.
- **Cross-contamination Guard:** NO references to news, breaking news, or CRP.
