# CurrentBrief (TikTok CRP)

## Goal
Optimize for completion rate, watch time, and engagement. Qualified For You feed views pay.

## Rules
- **Length:** STRICTLY 60 seconds minimum (required for CRP). 60-90s is the sweet spot.
- **Content:** Faceless AI-generated news videos. Factual and verified only.
- **Hook:** Within 3 seconds, stops the scroll.
- **SEO/Hashtags:** Fixed tags #news #breakingnews #newsupdate.
- **Disclosure:** AI Generated toggle MUST be ON.
- **Cross-contamination Guard:** NEVER use affiliate links, CTA to buy, or shop language.
