Seal Notes

This station assumes rescue artifacts are stored under the local workspace and that Azure reads may already be partially sealed off.

  • Rescue zips are expected under .artifacts/sealed.
  • The backend is throttled to one worker by default.
  • Port 5143 fronts both the local terminal page and the proxied backend API.
  • run-tsgo is present as an on-demand compose tool, not a permanently hot process.
