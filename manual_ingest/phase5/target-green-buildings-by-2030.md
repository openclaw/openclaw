# 80% green buildings by 2030

Singapore Green Plan 2030 target under [[climate/pillars/energy-reset|Energy Reset]]: have 80% of buildings by gross floor area be green buildings by 2030, using Green Mark as the policy frame.

Source: [Singapore Green Plan 2030](https://www.greenplan.gov.sg) — [[climate/sources/singapore-green-plan-2030]]
