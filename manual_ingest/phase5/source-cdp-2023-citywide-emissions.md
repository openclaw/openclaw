# CDP 2023 City-wide Emissions

Public CDP Open Data Portal dataset used for citywide Scope 1 and Scope 2 emission profiles.

Dataset URL: https://data.cdp.net/resource/u5an-e63y

API query used for the candidate city verification:
https://data.cdp.net/resource/u5an-e63y.json

Captured city records:
- Bangkok Metropolitan Administration, Thailand: inventory year 2020; Scope 1 16,915,719 metric tonnes CO2e; Scope 2 17,600,260 metric tonnes CO2e; population in year covered 5,588,222.
- Jakarta City Government, Indonesia: inventory year 2023; Scope 1 19,008,741.81 metric tonnes CO2e; Scope 2 0 metric tonnes CO2e; population in year covered 10,609,681.
- The Local Government of Quezon City, Philippines: inventory year 2018; Scope 1 4,154,187 metric tonnes CO2e; Scope 2 3,575,270 metric tonnes CO2e; population in year covered 3,040,672.
