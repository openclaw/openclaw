# Singapore Green Plan 2030

Official source page for the Singapore Green Plan 2030 pillars and headline targets captured in this Phase 5 ingest.

URL: https://www.greenplan.gov.sg

Captured Green Plan pillars:
- City in Nature
- Energy Reset
- Sustainable Living
- Green Economy
- Resilient Future

Captured headline targets:
- City in Nature: 1 million more trees; expanded nature parks.
- Energy Reset: at least 2 GWp solar by 2030; 60,000 EV charging points by 2030; 80% green buildings by 2030; all new cars cleaner-energy from 2030.
- Sustainable Living: 30% landfill waste reduction by 2030; at least 20% of schools carbon-neutral by 2030.
- Green Economy: Enterprise Sustainability Programme; green finance; CCUS research and development.
- Resilient Future: 30-by-30 local nutritional needs production; coastal and sea-level-rise defences.
